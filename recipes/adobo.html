Adobo Recipe

Adobo

Adobo is one of the most iconic and beloved Filipino dishes, known for its rich, savory flavor and simple ingredients. Traditionally made with chicken or pork, it’s simmered in a flavorful blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper. The result is tender, juicy meat with a slightly tangy and garlicky sauce that pairs perfectly with steamed rice. Whether served at family meals or special occasions, adobo is a true taste of Filipino comfort food.

Indgredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic (crushed)
  • Bay leaves
  • Whole black pepper
  • Water
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Sauté the garlic
  2. Add the meat
  3. Add seasonings
  4. Simmer
  5. Adjust and serve
