Garlic Shrimp Recipe

Garlic shrimp is a quick and flavorful dish made with tender shrimp sautéed in butter, oil, and lots of garlic. Infused with a hint of salt, pepper, and sometimes a splash of lemon, this simple recipe brings out the natural sweetness of the shrimp. It’s perfect as a main dish, appetizer, or even a topping for pasta or rice. With just a few ingredients, garlic shrimp is a delicious and easy meal ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Garlic (minced)
  • butter
  • Salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the shrimp
  2. Sauté the garlic
  3. Cook the shrimp
  4. Optional: Add lemon
  5. Serve hot
